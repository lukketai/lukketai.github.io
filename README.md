# Lukket AI

[lukket.ai](https://lukket.ai) - *Lukket* means /closed in Norwegian. Ironically, much of what we build will be open.

## What we do

### Small Language Models
We build compact, efficient language models that can be finetuned for specific verticals, giving teams powerful AI that fits their domain without the overhead of general-purpose giants.

### IDEs for Non-Programmers
We build intelligent development environments designed for domain experts, not software engineers.

- **Arth**: IDE for stock analysis. Research, model, and reason about markets with AI built in.
- **Shastra** *(in development)*: IDE for journalists and editors. Write better, faster, with AI that understands the craft.
